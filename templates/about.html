ABOUT US PAGE

Art Exposure was founded to commemorate the memory of Late Artist Kalyan Kumar Bose by his daughter Mrs. Uma Mitra.

There is no dearth of talented artists in our contemporary times who are deeply rooted to their culture and Art Exposure focuses on showcasing their works.

Designed to evolve over time, Art Exposure hosts multiple curated exhibitions to showcase new talent as well as eminent masters of regional, national and international repute.
